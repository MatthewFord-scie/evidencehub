Engagement survey

Understanding how the Evidence Hub can support you

Thank you for taking the time to provide feedback on SCIE's social care equity Evidence Hub. This survey is for people who draw on care and support and unpaid carers.

The questions in this survey were co-produced with the Evidence Hub’s advisory group. Your answers will help us understand how the Hub is used in practice, how clear and relevant it feels, and how it can better reflect real-life experiences of social care.

By completing this survey, you consent to your responses being held by the SCIE Research Team for approximately 12 months. We will not publish your name or contact details.
