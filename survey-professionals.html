Engagement survey

Understanding how the evidence hub can support your work

Thank you for taking the time to provide feedback on SCIE's social care equity Evidence Hub.
This survey asks about how you may use the Hub, how clear and relevant you find the content, what may be missing, and how you would like to engage with the Hub in the future.

The questions in this survey were co-produced with the Evidence Hub’s advisory group, bringing together perspectives from across social care.

By completing this survey, you consent to your responses being held by the SCIE Research Team for approximately 12 months. We will not publish your name or contact details.
